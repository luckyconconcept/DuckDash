# Duck & Dash Asset Prompts

Diese Prompts sind fuer neue Spielgrafiken gedacht. Ziel: klare Silhouetten, mobile Lesbarkeit und gleicher Cartoon-Stil wie die bestehenden Assets.

## 1. Zahnbuerste im Becher, Dive-Hindernis

Prompt:

```text
Create a polished 2D mobile game asset of a turquoise toothbrush cup floating on bath water with a colorful toothbrush sticking out diagonally. Friendly high-quality cartoon style, glossy plastic, soft highlights, playful bathroom theme, strong readable silhouette for a side-scrolling runner game. The cup should be the main visible object, with water splashes at the bottom and the toothbrush leaning upward. Bright cyan cup, pink and white toothbrush head, small bubbles. Transparent background, centered composition, no text, no shadow cut off, 1024x1024 PNG.
```

Purpose:
Dive-Hindernis. Der Spieler soll sofort verstehen: nicht draufspringen, sondern darunter tauchen.

## 2. Shampoo-Flasche, Stomp-Hindernis

Prompt:

```text
Create a polished 2D mobile game asset of a chunky purple shampoo bottle bobbing on bath water, friendly cartoon style, glossy plastic, big readable shape, foam and small splashes around the base. It should look safe to jump on in a side-scrolling runner game, with a flat-ish cap/top surface. Bright purple bottle with yellow cap, no text or only a simple generic label shape, transparent background, centered, no cropped edges, 1024x1024 PNG.
```

Purpose:
Stomp-Hindernis. Bringt mehr Variation als Seife, ohne eine neue Regel einzufuehren.

## 3. Quietschender Badeenten-Ring, Bonus-Lane

Prompt:

```text
Create a polished 2D mobile game asset of a small inflatable duck swim ring floating on bath water, cute cartoon mobile game style, glossy rubber, bright yellow duck head with orange beak attached to a blue ring, water ripples and bubbles at the base. Strong readable silhouette, transparent background, centered, no text, no cropped edges, 1024x1024 PNG.
```

Purpose:
Optionales Bonus-Objekt oder visuelle Markierung fuer sichere Perlenpfade.

## 4. Rasierer, Spaetes Risiko-Hindernis

Prompt:

```text
Create a polished 2D mobile game asset of a cartoon safety razor floating on bath water, playful but clearly hazardous, rounded edges, bright coral handle and silver-blue razor head, small water splash at the base. High-quality mobile game art, strong silhouette for a side-scrolling runner, not scary or realistic, transparent background, centered, no text, 1024x1024 PNG.
```

Purpose:
Spaetes High-Risk-Hindernis. Nur einsetzen, wenn die Grundmechanik stabil genug ist.

## 5. Goldene Mini-Gummiente, Rare Collectible

Prompt:

```text
Create a tiny golden rubber duck collectible for a colorful mobile game, cute glossy cartoon style, strong silhouette, sparkling highlights, small bubbles around it, transparent background, centered, no text, no cropped edges, 1024x1024 PNG.
```

Purpose:
Seltener +100 Collectible fuer Risiko-Routen und Combo-Momente.
